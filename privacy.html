Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Party Next Door Effective Date: 6/21/26 Party Next Door (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information when using our iOS app, Party Next Door. 1. Information We Collect We collect the following types of information: - Name - Email address - Username - Account authentication credentials managed by Firebase Authentication - Optional profile photo - Event details you create, such as titles, descriptions, images, and locations (all optional) - Usage data and interaction analytics via Firebase Analytics - Location data (only if explicitly allowed by the user) 2. How We Use Your Information We use the collected information to: - Allow account creation and sign-in using Firebase Authentication - Personalize your experience and display profile-related content - Enable event creation, sharing, and interaction features - Improve our app through usage analytics (e.g., Firebase Analytics) - Communicate with you if necessary for account-related issues 3. Authentication and Data Storage User accounts are managed through Firebase Authentication with email and password credentials. Data such as usernames, events, and profiles are stored securely using Firebase Firestore. Firebase may also store optional images through Firebase Storage. 4. Password Recovery If a user forgets their password, they may request a password reset email directly through the app. This service is managed securely through Firebase Authentication. 5. Analytics We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This helps us improve features and performance. Analytics data is collected anonymously and does not include sensitive information like your password. 6. Data Deletion and User Rights You can delete your account and data at any time. Account deletion removes your Firebase Authentication record and associated Firestore data. To request account deletion or data removal, please contact us at: partynextdoorcustomerservice@gmail.com or delete your account from the user screen. 7. Minors and Children’s Privacy This app is intended for users aged 13 and up. We do not knowingly collect or store personal data from children under the age of 13. If we become aware of a user under this age, we may delete their account to comply with privacy laws. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the app, please contact us. 8. Data Retention We retain your information only as long as your account is active or as needed for legitimate business purposes. Once an account is deleted, associated data will be removed from Firebase services. 9. International Users This app is available globally wherever the App Store and Firebase services are available. By using the app, you agree to the processing and storage of your information in accordance with this policy and applicable privacy laws. 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated within the app or via email. 11.
